Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

How quickly can I get my landing page?

We deliver most landing pages within 1-3 business days. The exact timeline depends on the complexity of your request and our current workload.

Do you offer custom domain hosting?

Our base package includes hosting on a subdomain (yourcompany.thechillcreatives.com). If you need a custom domain, we can help you set it up for an additional fee.

Can I update my landing page after it's built?

Yes! We offer maintenance plans that include updates. You can choose from monthly, quarterly, or annual plans based on your needs.

What do I need to provide for my landing page?

We'll need your content (text, images), brand colors (if you have them), and any specific features you want included like contact forms or special buttons.

Do you offer refunds?

We offer revisions to make sure you're happy with your landing page. If you're not satisfied with the initial design, we'll work with you to make it right.

Can you help with SEO for my landing page?

Yes! We optimize all our landing pages for search engines with proper heading structure, meta tags, and mobile responsiveness to help your page rank better for an additional fee.
